## Why Areg Exists

Many C++ projects fail not on algorithms, they fail on threads, IPC, and fragile wiring. Areg solves this:
- **Auto-managed concurrency & messaging** ‚Äì threads, services, and routing handled automatically  
- **Unified async API** ‚Äì RPC, Pub/Sub, and service discovery behave consistently across threads, processes, devices  
- **Resilient & observable** ‚Äì dynamic restarts, logging, and monitoring built-in
